Description

Abyssal Tide is a plunge into uncharted depths, where absolute silence and hidden power reign. This fragrance is like the thick velvet of a midnight ocean, harboring a mystery. It unfolds slowly, warming the skin with tart woody accords and a light, salty haze.

Olfactory Pyramid

  • Top Notes: Sea salt, black pepper, bergamot.
  • Heart Notes: Oud, cedarwood, incense.
  • Base Notes: Amber, vetiver, dark musk.

Who it's for

Ideal for those seeking a complex, status-defining, and hypnotic fragrance for special occasions.
